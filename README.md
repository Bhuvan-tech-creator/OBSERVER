# Journey from the Center of Da Earth (JFTCODE)

https://rajkumarthota.itch.io/jftcode

There is a miner named bob who got abandoned and is trying to get to the surface to exact his revenge. help bob get to the surface by controlling his drill and firing stalactites at the miners to help bob.
